Introduction:

Greed is a dice game where each player competes to be the first to reach 10,000 points.

Take risks, push the limit, and get Greedy!

Gameplay:

  • Step 1:
    • Roll 6 dice
    • Jump to Step 2
  • Step 2:
    • If all the dice form a scoring combination (see Scoring Rules below),
      • Add that score to the cumulative score
      • Jump to Step 1
    • If some of the dice contain a scoring combination
      • Jump to Step 3
    • If none of the dice form a scoring combination,
      • Clear the cumulative score
      • Pass 6 dice to the next player
      • Jump to Step 1
  • Step 3:
    • If the player does not have any points on the board (first scoring move):
      • You must score at least 500 points in a single turn to get on the board.
      • If your cumulative play score is less than 500 points, your turn ends with no points added.
      • If your cumulative play score is 500 or more, you may add it to your total and continue as normal.
    • Otherwise (if you already have points on the board):
      • Add the cumulative score to your total score, then:
        • You may bank any amount of points, regardless of total.
        • Pass the non-scoring dice to the next player
        • Pass the cumulative score to the next player
        • Jump to Step 4
      • Or continue rolling and risk your points (Jump to Step 5)
  • Step 4:
    • Decide whether to:
      • Clear the cumulative score and roll 6 Dice
      • Keep the cumulative score and roll the previous player's dice
    • Jump to Step 2
  • Step 5:
    • Decide which of the scoring dice you would like to:
      • Add to the cumulative score
      • Keep and roll again
    • Roll the remaining dice
    • Jump to Step 2

Scoring Rules:

  • 1's: One 1 = 100 points, Two 1's = 200 points, Three 1's = 300 points
  • 5's: One 5 = 50 points, Two 5's = 100 points, Three 5's = 500 points
  • 2's: Three 2â€™s = 200 points,
  • 3's: Three 3's = 300 points
  • 4's: Three 4's = 400 points
  • 6's: Three 6's = 600 points
  • Four of a kind = 1000 points
  • Five of a kind = 2000 points.
  • Six of a kind = 3000 points.
  • Three pairs = 1500 points.
  • A straight with all six dice = 2000 points.
