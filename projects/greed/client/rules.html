Introduction:

Greed is a dice game where each player competes to be the first to reach 10,000 points.

Take risks, push the limit, and get Greedy!

Gameplay:

  • Step 1:
    • Roll 6 dice
    • Jump to Step 2
  • Step 2:
    • If all the dice form a scoring combination (see Scoring Rules),
      • Add that score to the cumulative score
      • Jump to Step 1
    • If some of the dice contain a scoring combination
      • Jump to Step 3
    • Otherwise,
      • Clear the cumulative score
      • Pass 6 dice to the next player
      • Jump to Step 1
  • Step 3:
    • If the cumulative play score is greater than or equal to 500 points, decide whether to:
      • Add the cumulative score to your total score
        • Pass the non-scoring dice to the next player
        • Pass the cumulative score to the next player
        • Jump to Step 4
      • Jump to Step 5
    • Otherwise:
      • Jump to Step 5
  • Step 4:
    • Decide whether to:
      • Clear the cumulative score and roll 6 Dice
      • Keep the cumulative score and roll the previous player's dice
    • Jump to Step 2
  • Step 5:
    • Decide which of the scoring dice you would like to:
      • Add to the cumulative score
      • Keep and roll again
    • Roll the remaining dice
    • Jump to Step 2
